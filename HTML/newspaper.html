Student Newspaper

RTTP games require students to complete a significant amount of written work. In many games, these writing assignments manifest themselves as newspaper articles or blogs. Essentially, RTTP games want students to read each other's publications and react to them in character. This can be difficult when the documents are spread via physical copies or circulated through email chains. This website addresses this common problem by providing a central hub for students to upload their documents and read the work their peers produce.

In the fully-fledged version of the site, the form below will allow the student to upload their document to the Google Drive.
