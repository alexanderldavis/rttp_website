Daily Overview

RTTP games require a significantly larger amount of organization than an ordinary class in order to be successful. Students must prepare their assignments and be ready to debate on the appropriate day; failing to do so can result in the loss of game points for themselves and their faction. By digitizing and providing a detailed calendar of events, students can keep track of relevant due dates.
