Character Profile

An important component of RTTP games are the character profiles. These provide students with all of the information required for them to begin playing the game. Profiles establish key character objectives and provide strategy advice for the students. Some of these strategy suggestions benefit the player by undermining other players, so it is important to keep students from reading character profiles that do not belong to them.

In the fully-fledged version of the site, the plugin below will require the student to enter their NorseKey before accessing the document.
